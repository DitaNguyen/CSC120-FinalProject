The Sapphic Matcha Bar: Cheat Sheet
Available Commands
go [north/south]: Move between the Alley, the Main Bar, and the Storage Closet.
take [item]: Pick up necessary items (like the key or matcha).
look: Re-examine your current room, items, and the customer at the counter.
serve: Prepare and hand over a matcha to the current customer.
inventory (or i): View what you are currently carrying in your apron.
map: Display a visual layout of the cafe.
help: List all commands while in-game.
quit: Exit the game.

Game World Layout
The game world is arranged on a vertical axis:
Storage Closet (North): Contains the Ceremonial Matcha. This room is initially locked.
Main Matcha Bar (Center): The starting point. This is where patrons arrive and where you serve them.
Alleyway (South): A dark area behind the shop. You must go here first to find the dropped key.

Challenges & Mechanics
The Locked Door: You cannot enter the Storage Closet to get the matcha until you have the key from the Alleyway. If you try to enter without the key, the game will prompt you to go find it.

The Inventory Check: You cannot serve a customer until you have physically gone to the Storage Closet and used the take matcha command.

The Man Tax (Core Mechanic): The game’s main challenge/theme is the economic disparity in pricing. Male Customers are automatically billed $25.00, while Sapphic Customers are billed $6.50. You must serve the entire line to "win" the shift and maximize cafe funds.
